Hanna Michael Grow-Morales

Hanna was born in Chengdu, China on August 13, 1996. She was adopted by Patty Grow, a Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP) and by Carlos Morales. Hanna attended high school at Ball High School, in Galveston, TX from 2010-2014. She continued her education at Texas A&M University. Here she pursued a degree in Sport Management and a minor in business. Hanna completed her college degree December 15, 2018 with a B.S. in Sport Management & a minor in Business. She has relocated to Fort Collins, CO and is looking to pursue a career in college athletics. In her new found free time, she enjoy spending time with her 8 month old puppy Koda. Koda is a German Shepherd-Husky mix and loves exploring their new surroundings.
